How OZU works

Build your own private (or public) video search and creation space

Run OZU's models locally and invite collaborators into your own private OZU space. Explore shared spaces from other OZU users. Help contribute to a people-powered video world. The story is yours.

Feel like a filmmaker while you explore

Inspired by how it feels to bring films to life - OZU turns searching through video into creative flow. We make it fun and focused to dig through massive amounts of video. It's a blend of watching and discovery. Searching and curating. Usings all the elements of story - characters, themes, dialogue, locations, cinematography...

Use OZU for work and for play.

Filmmaking, remixed

What's possible when you integrate search and editing into something fun and simple? Join us and find out. It's time for a new generation of creative tools to help remix filmmaking for the internet age.

Want to use OZU with a traditional video workflow? Use OZU's Mac App to move to/from video editing software.
